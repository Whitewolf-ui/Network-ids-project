What it does

Built for the moment before things go wrong

A lightweight IDS that runs on your own hardware, so nothing you capture ever leaves your machine.

Port scan detection

Flags any source touching multiple ports in a short window — the classic signature of reconnaissance.

Sensitive port monitoring

Watches SSH, Telnet, RDP, MSSQL and MySQL, and alerts the instant one gets a connection attempt.

Live dashboard

A running feed of alerts, trend charts and top offenders — no more tailing a log file.

Self-hosted, always private

Runs entirely on your own machine. Your traffic and alert history stay with you.
